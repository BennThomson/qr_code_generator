What is a QR Code?

QR (Quick Response) Code is a two-dimensional barcode that stores information in a machine-readable format. It typically appears as a square pattern of black dots and squares on a white background. QR codes can contain links, text, contact details, or other data that can be quickly accessed by scanning them with a smartphone or QR scanner.

What is QR Code Generator

A QR Code generator is a tool that allows you to create QR (Quick Response) codes, which are two-dimensional barcodes that can store information such as website URLs, text, contact details, Wi-Fi credentials, and much more. These codes can be scanned using a QR code scanner or a smartphone camera, instantly providing the stored data.
